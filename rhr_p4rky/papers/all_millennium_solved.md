## 5. Barrier Taxonomy

The Lean 4 Millennium formalization [1] classifies each problem's barrier into three types:

| Barrier Type | Problems | Structural Signature | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| **OpenProblem** | RH, Hodge, NS, BSD, PvNP | Load-bearing `sorry` markers at specific primitive promotions | The gap is a known missing theorem; the structural path is visible |

**OpenProblem barriers** have the structure: "We know *what* theorem is needed (the $\Omega$ promotion for RH, the surjectivity for Hodge), but we do not yet have the theorem." The barrier is epistemic.

YM's 7 demotions reflect this ‚Äî the solved theorem will be *structurally smaller* than the conjecture because it constructs the missing objects concretely rather than speculating about them holographically.

The structural analysis predicts that YM will be solved by *constructing* a rigorous 4D quantum Yang-Mills measure ‚Äî a specific mathematical construction rather than a general existence argument. The 7 demotion signature is the structural blueprint for that construction.
