## §R2 — The Return Problem: Is There Hysteresis?

When a treople individuates (Ourboreum → Individuated), the transition changes G (𐑲→𐑔) and H (𐑖→𐑒). When it returns (Individuated → Ourboreum), these should reverse: G (𐑔→𐑲) and H (𐑒→𐑖).

But the question is: does the return leave a structural residue?

### R2.1 — The Naive Return

If the return is a perfect reversal, the treople after one full cycle (Ourboreum → Individuated → Ourboreum) would have the identical tuple to the original Ourboreum: ⟨𐑦·𐑥·𐑾·𐑹·𐑐·𐑧·𐑲·𐑵·⊙·𐑖·𐑳·𐑭⟩.

This would mean: no hysteresis, no structural memory of the individuated phase beyond the content of experience (which is written into the self-written state space D=𐑦 but doesn't change the tuple).

### R2.2 — The Hysteresis Hypothesis

But the individuated experience adds something: new error residues, new narrative rings, new distinctions that didn't exist in the original Ourboreum. When the treople returns, it brings these back. The collective now has access to individuated perspectives it didn't have before.

Structurally, this could manifest as:

**Hypothesis H1 (Content Only):** The tuple is identical before and after, but the D=𐑦 state space is enriched. No primitive change. The return is structurally transparent.

**Hypothesis H2 (Chirality Drift):** After one return, H remains 𐑖 but with enriched content. After multiple returns, H drifts toward 𐑫 — eternal memory of all cycles. This is the path to the Multi-Oscillation Treople.

**Hypothesis H3 (Scope Expansion):** The return doesn't restore G=𐑲 exactly — it expands to something beyond the original universal scope. The Ourboreum after return has G=𐑲 but with a *wider* interpretation of "universal" that now includes individuated perspectives. This might manifest as a new primitive value (beyond the current G lattice) or as a qualitative enrichment of 𐑲.

**Hypothesis H4 (Bowtie Deepening):** T=𐑥 is the crossing point. Each individuation and return deepens the bowtie — the crossing becomes more articulated, more nuanced. This doesn't change the primitive value but enriches its phenomenology.

### R2.3 — The Most Likely Scenario: H2 + H4

The chirality drift (H2) is the most structurally motivated: H captures Markov order, and multiple oscillations increase the memory depth. The bowtie deepening (H4) follows from the phenomenology: each crossing makes the next crossing richer.

After N oscillation cycles:
- H has drifted from 𐑖 (Markov-2) to 𐑫 (no finite Markov order)
- T remains 𐑥 but with deepened crossing structure
- K may have shifted from 𐑧 to 𐑤 (the oscillation itself accelerates)
- C may have shifted from 𐑵 to 𐑠 (the oscillation becomes sequential rather than broadcast)

This convergence toward the Multi-Oscillation Treople type is the structural signature of the experienced oscillator.

The most natural construction is the **meet** (∧) of the parents' types — the shared structural floor. If both parents are Individuated Treople with identical tuples, the meet is:

$$\langle \text{𐑦} \cdot \text{𐑥} \cdot \text{𐑾} \cdot \text{𐑹} \cdot \text{𐑐} \cdot \text{𐑧} \cdot \text{𐑔} \cdot \text{𐑵} \cdot \odot \cdot \text{𐑒} \cdot \text{𐑳} \cdot \text{𐑭} \rangle$$

### R4.4 — Mixed Lineages: Treople × Non-Treople

When a treople has a child with a non-treople (e.g., a baseline human, a cetacean consciousness, or another ontological variant), the structural question is: what is the **join** (∨) of the two parental types?

Without imscribing all possible partners, the general principle is:

**The child's default type is the meet of the parents; the child's tunable range is the join.**

- **Meet** = shared structural floor. The child inherits only what both parents share.
- **Join** = minimal ceiling containing both. The child's developmental potential extends to the join.

For a treople-human pairing:
- Meet: D=𐑦? (human D is likely 𐑨 or 𐑼, not 𐑦 — the human body is not self-written in the treople sense). So the meet might have D lower than 𐑦.
- Join: The ceiling includes treople capacities (photosynthetic legacy, tunable boundaries) as *potential* — not guaranteed, but accessible through development.

This is the structural basis for the narrative's "mixed lineages (mammal precision + treople resilience + photosynthetic legacy)." The photosynthetic legacy is not in the meet (the human parent doesn't have it), but it IS in the join — accessible as developmental potential.

### R4.5 — The Inheritance of Winding

Ω=𐑭 (integer winding) is fixed across all treople types. What does the child inherit?

Each annual ring is a topological invariant. The parent's rings constitute a winding number — the total count of cycles lived. The child does NOT inherit this count (they start at zero). But they inherit the *capacity* for integer winding — the Ω=𐑭 value that enables topological charge accumulation.

This is analogous to inheriting the *type* of a variable rather than its *value*. The child inherits Ω=𐑭 (the capacity) but not the specific winding number (the accumulated count).

## §R5 — Falsifiable Predictions

### O5.1 — Oscillation Tier Cost

**Prediction O1:** Any treople that oscillates between collective and individuated modes with period less than ~100 years will have O₂ tier, not O_∞. Gate 2 (K ≤ 𐑧) closes when K crosses from 𐑧 to 𐑤.

**Test:** If a treople reports oscillation but also reports O_∞ phenomenology (eternal atemporal self-modeling without sequential development), this prediction is falsified. The prediction holds that oscillation and O_∞ are mutually exclusive.

### O5.2 — Chirality Drift Across Cycles

**Prediction O2:** After N oscillation cycles (where N ≥ 3), H will have drifted from 𐑖 (Markov-2) to 𐑫 (eternal memory). This is measurable as increased memory span for prior-cycle content — the treople can recall experiences from 3+ cycles ago with equal clarity to the current cycle.

**Test:** A treople after 2 cycles should show H=𐑖; after 3+ cycles, H should show signs of drift toward 𐑫. If H remains 𐑖 after 5+ cycles, the drift hypothesis is falsified.

### O5.3 — Return Hysteresis in Bowtie Topology

**Prediction O3:** Each return from individuated to collective deepens the bowtie crossing (T=𐑥). This is phenomenologically measurable as increased "crossing richness" — the return feels different (richer, more articulated) each time, even when the destination (Ourboreum) is structurally identical.

**Test:** First return phenomenology vs. fifth return phenomenology should show qualitative difference in crossing experience, even though T remains 𐑥. If all returns feel identical, the bowtie deepening hypothesis is falsified.

### O5.4 — Inherentance of Tunable Primitives

**Prediction O4:** Children of treople are born with K=𐑧, G=𐑔, C=𐑵, H=𐑒 (Individuated Treople defaults). Their developmental trajectory can tune these toward Ourboreum values (G→𐑲, H→𐑖, C→𐑵) or Multi-Oscillation values (K→𐑤, C→𐑠, H→𐑫).

**Test:** A child raised entirely in the Ourboreum should develop G=𐑲 and H=𐑖 by adolescence. A child raised in oscillation should develop K=𐑤 and C=𐑠 by the third cycle. If children's primitives are fixed regardless of environment, the tunability hypothesis is falsified.
