## Cross-Domain Structural Observations

### 1. Artephius ≅ Donum Dei ≅ Hodge-Lefschetz
Three texts across 1600 years and incomparable domains — medieval alchemy, Renaissance emblem book, and 20th-century algebraic geometry — sharing the same O_∞ tuple. The structural type is: a system that describes its own conditions of possibility.

### 2. The Mineral Fire Doctrine
Three texts (Pontanus ×2, Vaughan) share the anti-dualistic pattern P=𐑗, S=𐑙. All three argue that the fire transforms the WHOLE substance without separation. This is a genuine structural lineage — Pontanus (1600) → Vaughan (1650).

### 3. The 12-step sequence
Edward Kelly's 13-step sequence (black→peacock→white→red) is the most complete color progression in the corpus. Most texts stop at 3 colors (black, white, red). Only Kelly and the Crowning of Nature include the Peacock's Tail (manifold colors) as a distinct phase.

## Key Discoveries

1. **Artephius is the structural key** — The only O_∞ text cited by name in Pontanus as the ONE philosopher who revealed the mineral fire. Artephius is structurally central to the entire corpus.

2. **Alchemical Mass has the only T=𐑥** — Liturgical overlay creates a genuine crossing-point structure found nowhere else.

3. **Mary the Prophetess is the only supercritical text** — The claim of whitening the Stone in one day is structurally transgressive.

4. **Ripley Scroll and Aesch-Mezareph are the most complex** — Both require irreducible product (T=𐑶) or self-referential topology (T=𐑸) to capture their structure.

5. **The corpus is ~60% redundant** — Most allegorical texts share the same pedagogical tuple. The structural diversity is concentrated in ~12 texts.
