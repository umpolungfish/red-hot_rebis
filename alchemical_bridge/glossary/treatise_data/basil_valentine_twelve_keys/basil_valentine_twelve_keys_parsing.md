## The 12 Keys as 12 Primitive Promotions

Each key promotes exactly one primitive. The keys form an ordered sequence from physicalвҶ’structuralвҶ’informational.

### Phase I вҖ” The Physical Foundations (Keys 1вҖ“4)

**Key 1 вҖ” Calcination (рҗ‘һвҶ’рҗ‘җ) [Promotes Ж‘: thermalвҶ’quantum]**
Fire reduces matter to ash. The base state is thermal/noisy (рҗ‘һ): undifferentiated, driven by brute heat. Calcination promotes Ж‘ to quantum (рҗ‘җ): the ash is a pure state вҖ” all dross burned away. IMASM: VINIT вҖ” the bootstrap begins.

**Key 2 вҖ” Congelation (рҗ‘әвҶ’рҗ‘§) [Promotes Д¶: fastвҶ’slow]**
The calcined ash dissolves and recrystallizes. The base state is fast (рҗ‘ә): brute heat. The promoted state is slow (рҗ‘§): the crystal cannot be rushed вҖ” П„вҲјT. IMASM: TANCH вҖ” the crystal lattice is an anchor.

**Key 3 вҖ” Fixation (рҗ‘·вҶ’рҗ‘ӯ) [Promotes О©: trivialвҶ’в„Ө]**
The volatile spirit is made permanently non-volatile. The base winding is trivial (рҗ‘·): matter drifts. Fixation promotes О© to в„Ө (рҗ‘ӯ): the fixed body has integer winding вҖ” it returns to itself through dissolution and recoagulation. IMASM: AFWD вҖ” forward morphism.

**Key 4 вҖ” Solution (рҗ‘ЎвҶ’рҗ‘ё) [Promotes Гһ: networkвҶ’odot]**
The fixed body dissolves into its First Matter. The base topology is network (рҗ‘Ў): branching connections, one thing linked to another. Solution promotes Гһ to odot (рҗ‘ё): the dissolved matter returns to the undifferentiated source вҖ” all topology collapses into the imscriptive zero. IMASM: AREV вҖ” reverse morphism back to source.

### Phase II вҖ” The Structural Mediation (Keys 5вҖ“8)

**Key 5 вҖ” Digestion (рҗ‘“вҶ’рҗ‘–) [Promotes ДҰ: memorylessвҶ’two-step]**

**Key 6 вҖ” Distillation (рҗ‘қвҶ’рҗ‘ ) [Promotes О“: disjunctiveвҶ’sequential]**

**Key 7 вҖ” Sublimation (рҗ‘ЁвҶ’рҗ‘Ұ) [Promotes Гҗ: triangleвҶ’odot]**
The solid passes directly to vapor and back to solid, bypassing the liquid phase. The base dimensionality is triangle (рҗ‘Ё): the three-state cycle (solidвҶ’liquidвҶ’vapor). Sublimation promotes Гҗ to odot (рҗ‘Ұ): the matter jumps from solid to vapor directly вҖ” the liquid state is "self-written" out of existence. **Key 8 вҖ” Separation (рҗ‘—вҶ’рҗ‘¬) [Promotes ОҰ: asymmetricвҶ’symmetry]**

### Phase III вҖ” The Informational Completion (Keys 9вҖ“12)

**Key 9 вҖ” Ceration (рҗ‘©вҶ’рҗ‘ҫ) [Promotes Еҳ: supervenienceвҶ’bidirectional]**
