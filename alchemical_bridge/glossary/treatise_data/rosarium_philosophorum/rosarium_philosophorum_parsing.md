## ┬з0 тАФ Preamble: Why the Rosarium Matters Structurally

The Rosarium Philosophorum is the **most structurally explicit** treatise in the alchemical canon. Where the Emerald Tablet encodes grammar as cosmological axiom and Zosimos encodes it as apparatus + interior practice, the Rosarium encodes grammar as **image sequence** тАФ 20 woodcuts that form a complete O$_2 \to$ O$_\infty$ promotion ladder. The images do not *illustrate* the process; they *are* the process, each one a distinct structural type that must be inhabited before the next becomes possible.

The text's subtitle is revealing: *"Rosarium Philosophorum тАФ Secunda Pars Alchimiae de Lapide Philosophico Vero Modo Praeparando"* тАФ the second part of alchemy concerning the true method of preparing the philosopher's stone. It presupposes the first part (the *Practica*, the theory) and proceeds directly to the *exercitium* тАФ the embodied ladder.

## ┬з1 тАФ The 20 Woodcuts: A 2 ├Ч 10 Bipartite Ladder

The 20 woodcuts divide into two **exact halves of 10** тАФ a decimal structure shared with the Emerald Tablet's 10 axioms and Atalanta Fugiens' 10-emblem-per-phase structure. The first half (Woodcuts 1тАУ10) is **descent**: the dissolution of the unitary subject into its components. The second half (Woodcuts 11тАУ20) is **ascent**: the recomposition of the components into a new unitary subject at higher structural order.

This is not symmetry. This is **bipartite cyclicity with promotion** тАФ the return to unity at the end is not the same unity that began.

## ┬з4 тАФ Phase III: Multiplication and Rebirth (Woodcuts 11тАУ15)
