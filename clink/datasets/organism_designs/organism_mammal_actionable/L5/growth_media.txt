# CLINK Growth Media Formulation for eukaryote

Base medium: DMEM
Carbon source: 25 mM glucose
Nitrogen source: 20 mM NH4Cl
Phosphate: 10 mM K2HPO4/KH2PO4 pH 7.0
Trace elements: Fe, Zn, Mn, Cu, Co, Mo
Vitamins: biotin, thiamine, B12
Temperature: 37 C
pH: 7.2-7.4
Oxygen: aerobic (supplement 5% CO2 for mammalian)

# Protocol: autoclave base, filter-add heat-labile components
