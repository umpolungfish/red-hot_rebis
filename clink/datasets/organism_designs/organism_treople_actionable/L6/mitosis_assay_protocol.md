# Mitosis Checkpoint Assay Protocol

## Cell Line: CLINK-designed (2n=46)

### Materials
- Cell culture: DMEM + 10% FBS + 1% P/S
- Nocodazole (100 ng/mL for spindle disruption)
- MG132 (10 uM for metaphase arrest)
- Anti-Aurora B antibody (1:500)
- DAPI (1 ug/mL)

### Procedure
1. Seed cells at 70% confluence on coverslips
2. Treat with nocodazole for 16 h to depolymerize microtubules
3. Fix with 4% PFA for 15 min at RT
4. Permeabilize with 0.1% Triton X-100
5. Block with 5% BSA for 1 h
6. Incubate with anti-Aurora B (1:500) overnight at 4C
7. Wash 3x with PBS
8. Incubate with Alexa Fluor 488 secondary (1:1000)
9. Mount with DAPI-containing medium
10. Image on confocal microscope (60x oil)

### Expected Results
- Prometaphase arrest: >80% rounded cells with condensed chromosomes
- Aurora-B at inner centromere in prometaphase
- Spindle checkpoint: active (Mad2 at kinetochores)
