# Epithelial Organoid Differentiation Protocol

### Day 0: Embedding
1. Dissociate cells with TrypLE for 5 min
2. Count and resuspend at 5000 cells/40 uL
3. Mix with 40 uL Matrigel (GFR, Corning)
4. Plate 80 uL droplets in pre-warmed 24-well plate
5. Incubate 30 min at 37C for gelation
6. Add 500 uL complete organoid medium

### Days 1-7: Expansion
- Change medium every 2 days
- Add ROCK inhibitor Y-27632 (10 uM) for first 3 days
- Expected: >80% organoid formation efficiency

### Days 7-14: Differentiation
- Remove Wnt3a, add differentiation factors
- Add 3 uM CHIR99021 (GSK3i) + 1 uM A83-01 (TGFbi)
- Monitor budding morphology

### Harvest
1. Remove medium, add 1 mL ice-cold PBS
2. Pipette to break Matrigel
3. Centrifuge 300g x 5 min
4. Proceed to RNA extraction or fixation
