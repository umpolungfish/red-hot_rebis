Cleanup phase G: consolidate packaging onto pyproject.toml, drop setup.py

setup.py and pyproject.toml duplicated the project metadata and entry points,
a standing maintenance hazard. pyproject.toml is the build backend, so it is
now the single source: ported setup.py's find_packages include list and
package_data into [tool.setuptools.packages.find] and
[tool.setuptools.package-data], and carried over the author and classifiers.
The archived experiment dirs (unified_demo, Ars_Therapeutica, plasma,
natural_products) are dropped from the include list.

Verified by building the wheel from pyproject alone: it discovers exactly the
16 wired packages with no archived leakage, ships all 18 console-script entry
points, and rebis.py status/verify stay green. setup.py removed.
